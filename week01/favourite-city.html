My Favourite Cities

Bellow is list of my favourite cities and what i like about each:

Freetown (Sierra Leone)

What I liked about Freetown

Freetown is its incredible resilience and warmth—despite its challenges, the city pulses with vibrant energy, stunning hillside ocean views, and a rich, welcoming Creole culture that makes you feel at home the moment you arrive.

Accra (Ghana)

What I liked about Accra

What I love about Accra is its electric, fast-paced energy—where bustling markets, lively street food scenes, vibrant art and music, and the warm, friendly spirit of its people come together against the backdrop of beautiful coastal sunsets, making it a city that feels both deeply authentic and endlessly exciting
